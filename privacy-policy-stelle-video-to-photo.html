Privacy Policy

Stelle VideoToPhoto — Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Stelle VideoToPhoto ("we", "our" or "the app") is a tool that allows you to extract frames from videos stored on your device and save them as photos to your gallery. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

2. Information We Access

The app requests access to your device's photo library and video files solely to allow you to select a video and save the extracted frame. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing is performed locally on your device.

3. Camera and Media Permissions

To function correctly, the app requires permission to access your photo library and media files. This permission is used exclusively to:

  • Let you select a video from your gallery.
  • Save the extracted frame back to your gallery.

You can revoke these permissions at any time from your device settings.

4. Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information. This data helps us identify and fix technical issues. The information collected includes device type, operating system version, and app version at the time of a crash. No personally identifiable information is collected through crash reporting.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. The only data transmitted externally is anonymous crash diagnostic information sent to Firebase Crashlytics for app stability purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or any form of personal data entry.
